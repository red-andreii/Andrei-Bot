Andreii Bot Privacy Policy


1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Andreii Bot. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Discord bot. By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.


2. Information We Collect

2.1. User Data

Username and User ID: We collect your Discord username and user ID for identifying you within our services.

Messages and Commands: We may collect the content of messages and commands you send to the Bot for processing and providing the desired functionality.

2.2. Server Data

Server ID: We collect server IDs to know which servers are using the Bot.

Channel Data: We collect channel IDs to know where the Bot should send responses.

2.3. Log Data

Logs: We may collect logs of interactions with the Bot to monitor and improve its performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1. To Provide and Improve the Service

We use the information collected to operate and improve the Bot's functionality.

3.2. To Communicate with Users

We may use your information to respond to inquiries, provide support, and update you on new features.

3.3. Compliance with Legal Obligations

We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to legal processes.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., hosting providers) that have access to your data. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

4.2. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes.

5. Data Security

We prioritize your data security. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


6. Your Rights

6.1. Access and Update Information

You have the right to access and update the information we hold about you.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the Bot after any changes signifies your acceptance of the new policy.
